Shipping FAQ

When will my order ship?

Most orders ship within 24 hours of placement. Custom builds may take 3-5 business days to prepare and ship.

Can I change my shipping address?

You can change your shipping address within 2 hours of placing your order by contacting our customer service team.

Do you ship on weekends?

We process orders Monday through Friday. Weekend orders will be processed and shipped on the next business day.

What if I'm not home for delivery?

Our courier partners will attempt delivery twice. If unsuccessful, they'll leave a notice with pickup instructions.

Is shipping insurance included?

Yes, all shipments include basic insurance. Additional coverage is available for high-value orders.

Can I pick up my order?

Yes, we offer free pickup from our Karachi showroom. Select "Store Pickup" during checkout.
